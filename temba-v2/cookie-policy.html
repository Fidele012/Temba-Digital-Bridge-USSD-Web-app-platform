Cookie Policy

Temba Digital Bridge  ·  Last updated: July 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit — such as your preferred language, authentication state, and session data — making it easier to use the platform on your next visit.

Temba Digital Bridge uses only the cookies necessary to make the web application function correctly. We do not use advertising, tracking, or third-party marketing cookies.

2. Cookies We Use

Temba Digital Bridge uses strictly necessary and functional cookies only. No third-party advertising or analytics cookies are used.
Cookie / Storage Key Purpose Duration
temba_session Stores your login session, name, role, and preferences (language, theme) in localStorage so you remain signed in between page loads. Until sign-out or browser data is cleared
temba_lang Remembers your chosen interface language (English or Kinyarwanda) across sessions. Persistent (localStorage)
temba_theme Remembers your preferred colour scheme (light or dark mode). Persistent (localStorage)
Access token (in-memory) A short-lived JWT used to authenticate API requests. Stored in memory only — never written to a cookie or localStorage. 30 minutes (expires automatically)
Refresh token (HTTP-only cookie) Used to silently renew your access token without requiring you to sign in again. Set as an HTTP-only, Secure, SameSite cookie so it cannot be read by JavaScript. 7 days

3. USSD Channel

The USSD channel (*384*36640#) operates entirely over the mobile network and does not use cookies or browser storage of any kind. Your USSD PIN and session data are processed server-side and are never stored in a browser.

4. Your Choices

Because Temba Digital Bridge uses only strictly necessary and functional cookies, the application will not function correctly if these are disabled. You can, however, clear your browser's local storage or cookies at any time through your browser settings, which will sign you out and reset your preferences.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear browsing data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search and services → Clear browsing data

On mobile devices, you can clear site data from your browser's site-settings menu.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time as the platform evolves. Any material changes will be communicated through an in-app notice or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
