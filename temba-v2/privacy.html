Privacy Policy

Effective date: 15 July 2026  |  Last updated: 15 July 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Who We Are

Temba Digital Bridge ("Temba", "we", "us") operates a digital platform for water service management in Rwanda. Our contact email is tembadigitalbridge@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Identity Full name, email address, phone number Account creation, contact, authentication
Location Province, district, sector, cell, village Matching you with local water service providers
Service data Reports, appointments, service requests, status history Providing and improving the service
Usage data Login timestamps, device type, browser, IP address Security, fraud prevention, analytics
USSD data Phone number, session activity (for feature-phone users) Providing offline USSD access to the platform
Preferences Notification settings (SMS, email, in-app), language Personalising your experience

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the service: match reports with providers, send notifications, track resolutions;
  • Communications: send SMS, email, and in-app alerts about your submitted issues, appointments, and service requests — and provider responses — based on your notification preferences;
  • Security: detect and prevent fraud, unauthorised access, and abuse;
  • Improvement: analyse aggregated, anonymised data to improve platform features;
  • Legal compliance: comply with Rwandan law and regulatory requirements.

4. SMS and Email Notifications

If you enable SMS notifications, your phone number is shared with our SMS gateway (Africa's Talking) solely for delivering notification messages. If you enable email notifications, your email address is used to send updates about your water service requests. You can opt out of any notification channel at any time in your account settings. Standard SMS carrier rates may apply.

5. Sharing Your Information

We share your information only as follows:

  • Water service providers: your name, phone number, district, and issue details are shared with the provider assigned to your request so they can contact you and deliver the service;
  • SMS gateway (Africa's Talking): your phone number and message content, solely to deliver SMS notifications;
  • Cloud infrastructure: we use Railway and PostgreSQL cloud services to store data securely;
  • Legal obligations: we may disclose data if required by Rwandan law, court order, or government authority.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Water service records (reports, appointments, service requests) are retained for a minimum of 3 years for accountability and regulatory purposes. You may request deletion of your account by emailing us; we will comply unless retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights

Under Rwanda's Law No. 058/2021 on the Protection of Personal Data and Privacy, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Correct inaccurate data;
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements);
  • Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes;
  • Withdraw consent for notification channels at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at tembadigitalbridge@gmail.com.

8. Security

We protect your data using:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit;
  • Bcrypt hashing for passwords and USSD PINs;
  • Role-based access controls to restrict who can view sensitive data;
  • Audit logs of all significant account and data actions.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

Temba uses browser local storage to maintain your login session and language preference. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking technologies.

10. Children's Privacy

Temba is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us to have the account removed.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you via email or in-app alert before material changes take effect. Continued use of the platform after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

For privacy enquiries or to exercise your rights:
